Why a kit

Since the project started, there has been several requests of how to build a complete CoderBot without having to purchase all of the components from several shops.

The kit as the objective of simplifyng the building of a complete CoderBot, fully based on the open source and open harware project.

What's in the kit

The kit contains all of the components required to build a CoderBot, the Raspberry, the chassis, motors an motor controller board: no soldering required.

  • Raspberry Pi Model B+
  • Camera Module
  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • Motor control and ultrasonic sensor "hat"
  • SD card with pre installed raspbian image
  • WiFi
  • Microphone
  • Amplified louspeaker
  • Power bank (Lithium battery, charger not included)
  • Cables
  • Motors
  • Wheels
  • Chassis (wood)
  • Screws and bolts
